What should my second paragraph be about? How about books? I just started re-reading Jonathan Strange and Mr. Norrell. It's a great book, you should try it.

What is it about, you ask? It's about a group of magicians in England, who now sit around and talk about magic. Except there are two magicians (can you guess what their names are?) who ask, "how come we don't actually do magic anymore?" And they set out to practice magic again for the first time in several hundred years. The book creates an alternate - magical- history of the world, and is dense with footnotes and references to this history. It's seriously great. TRY IT!

BBC recently made a show based on the book, but you should definitely try the book before you watch the movie. That's always best practice, no matter what the show is; if there's a book, read it first!
